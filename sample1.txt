'''Morcombelake''' (also spelled '''Morecombelake''') is a small village near [[Bridport]] in [[Dorset]], [[England]], within the ancient parish of [[Whitchurch Canonicorum]]. [[Golden Cap]], part of the [[Jurassic Coast]] World Heritage Site, is nearby.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nationaltrust.org.uk/golden-cap/|title=Golden Cap|publisher=National Trust|accessdate=2014-05-04}}</ref>
